Editorial Policy

How Kida Site Hub creates and updates publisher content.

Hub About Contact Privacy Editorial policy Terms

Editorial standard

Pages are written to explain a narrow data topic clearly. The site avoids fake reviews, copied articles, scraped filler, and claims that a metric is investment, travel, or gameplay advice.

Corrections

If a metric label, explanation, or page detail is wrong, the publisher reviews the issue and updates the generated page on the next production deploy.

Advertising separation

Advertising code is kept separate from the editorial explanation. Ads must not replace the core page content, and the site should remain useful even when no ads are served.
